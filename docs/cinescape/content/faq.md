## Booking

### When can I book tickets for new movies?

Starting from Wednesday of every week, you may book any movie released throughout that week.

### How do I book online?

After creating your Cinescape account, you can book tickets online using your registered Cinescape Club Card or a bank card.

### How many tickets can I book at once?

You can book up to 10 tickets per transaction.

### How do I collect my tickets after booking?

After your reservation is successful, head to the kiosk machine at the location of your booking, follow the on-screen instructions, and enter your booking code — your tickets will be printed.

### How do I confirm my booking succeeded?

Once your reservation succeeds, a confirmation message is displayed on your device. Cinescape also sends SMS and email confirmation to the contact information saved on your Cinescape profile.

### Can I book a whole theater for a birthday or private event?

Yes — you can book any theater for birthday parties or meetings. Contact the call center on **180-3456** for more info.

### Can the call center book on my behalf?

Yes — if you have a Cinescape Club Card or any credit card, call **180-3456** and an agent can help you book.

## Cancellation & Refunds

### Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes — cancellations are accepted up to **2 hours** before the show starts.

### How do refunds work?

- **Credit card payment:** the amount is credited to your Cinescape account (electronic balance / wallet) — **not** refunded to the original card.
- **Cinescape Club Card:** the amount is credited back to the Club Card.

> This matches the T&C: card refunds go to your wallet/loyalty balance, not back to the bank card, and that balance can be used for future reservations.

### When are refunds NOT given?

- If you arrive without required entry documents (civil ID where required).
- If Cinescape management asks you to leave for violating rules.
- For food orders once "Prepare Your Order" has been clicked (see T&C).

## Accessibility

### Is there wheelchair access?

Yes — there are wheelchair spaces at all Cinescape cinema locations.

### Is there any benefit for wheelchair users?

Yes — wheelchair users are eligible for a **lifetime free membership** with Cinescape.

> Free entry also applies when presenting a valid disability card issued by the Public Authority for the Disabled in Kuwait (see T&C).

## 4DX

### Who cannot watch 4DX?

Per Cinescape and per the T&C:

- Pregnant women
- People weighing **over 120 kg**
- Viewers with heart, back, or neck problems
- People with motion sickness
- The elderly

### Who can watch 4DX with conditions?

Children **over 4 years old** AND **at least 100 cm tall**.

### What should I know about the 4DX experience?

Seats have flashing lights, smoke and scents, strong vibrations, and water spray. **Hot beverages are not allowed** in 4DX theaters (they spill when the seats move).

## Age & Entry Rules

### What age rules apply?

In compliance with Ministry of Information resolutions **No. 30 of 2016** and **No. 3 of 2025**. Cinescape management has the right to check official photo ID before allowing a child to watch. Customers younger than the movie's stipulated age rating will **not** be admitted — and tickets are not refunded in that case.

### What IDs are accepted to verify age?

Civil ID, passport, or any official document containing a photograph of the child and indicating their age.

### Are there any free-entry exceptions for kids?

Children **under 3** get free entry on **Tuesdays only** at the **Cinescape Ajyal** branch (one child per parent, sharing the parent's seat, for G- or PG-rated films only). See T&C.

## Pricing & Promotions

### Are there any discount days?

**50% off every Monday** on all cinema experiences — excluding **VIP** and **Skyline**. (Source: T&C; the FAQ snippet does not state this explicitly but this is the canonical rule.)

### Can I upgrade my ticket?

Yes — tickets can be upgraded to a higher category (Regular → Premium → VIP) at any time before the show starts, subject to availability. **Not valid with any promotional offer.**

### Are there bank promotions?

Yes — NBK and Gulf Bank each run their own promotions with their own T&Cs. See:

- NBK: https://www.cinescape.com.kw/promotion/7
- Gulf Bank: https://www.cinescape.com.kw/promotion/11
